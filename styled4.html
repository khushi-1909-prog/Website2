Style Tips for Every Look

By Better Buy Style Experts

Everyday Casual Looks

Everyday kurtis look best with shoes that balance comfort and style. Ballet flats, Kolhapuris, loafers, and mules pair well with cotton and printed kurtis. White or pastel sneakers add a youthful, modern touch.

Traditional & Festive Outfits

For embroidered, silk, or festive kurtis, choose footwear that enhances elegance. Juttis, mojaris, embellished Kolhapuris, and subtle heeled sandals elevate traditional outfits beautifully for weddings and festivals.

Indo-Western & Modern Kurtis

Modern kurtis pair well with trendy footwear. Block heels, wedges, pointed flats, ankle boots, and strappy sandals help create a clean Indo-Western look that feels stylish and contemporary.

Choosing Footwear by Occasion

The right footwear depends on the event. Flats work best for office wear, ornate footwear suits weddings, sneakers or mules fit casual outings, and heels complete formal festive looks perfectly.

Conclusion

Whether casual, festive, or modern, the right footwear completes your kurti outfit. With thoughtful choices, each look appears polished, stylish, and confidence-boosting.
